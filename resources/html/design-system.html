05
Interaction

Motion Principles

All motion is deliberate, unhurried, and precise. Think luxury car commercial — not a social media ad. One primary easing curve governs everything.

Primary Easing

All entrance animations, transitions, and hover states use a single easing curve: slow start, decisive middle, soft deceleration.

cubic-bezier(0.25, 0, 0, 1)

Entrance Reveals

Elements enter from below with opacity fade. Never from the sides. Stagger children at 80–120ms intervals — never more than 6 children staggered at once.

translateY(20px) → (0) · opacity 0→1
duration: 0.8–1s · delay: +0.08s per child

Counter Animation

Statistics count up from zero on scroll entry. Use easeOutCubic — numbers rush up then ease precisely onto the target. Triggered by IntersectionObserver.

0
duration: 1.4–2s · ease: 1-(1-t)³

Scroll Parallax

Background layers move at 25–35% of scroll speed. Never exceed 40% — beyond that, the depth effect becomes nauseating rather than cinematic.

translateY(scrollY × 0.28)
requestAnimationFrame · passive listener

Gate / Parallax Reveal

The hero gate scales from 1→2.5 over the scroll range. Opacity fades to 0 in the final 38% of travel. Title fades out at 2.8× scroll speed (faster than the gate).

scale(1 + progress × 2.4)
opacity: 0 when progress > 0.62

Hover States

Cards lift by 3px on hover. Borders brighten. Internal radial glow fades in. Accent lines draw from left. All at 350ms — hovering should feel considered, not snappy.

translateY(-3px) · 0.35s
border-color · box-shadow · ::after width

The Golden Rule of Motion

Every animation should feel like something a human would do slowly and with intention — not a UI framework default. If an animation calls attention to itself, it's wrong. Motion here is felt, not noticed.

06
Visual Atmosphere

Lighting Language

Light is used to create depth, focus attention, and establish emotional tone. Three modes — each applied to a different context in the deck.

Warm Gold

Luxury · Retail sections. Warm amber radial from upper-right

Used in: Retail · Luxury · Fashion Avenue

Crown Light

Reverence · Authority. Soft top-down gold, fades to void

Used in: Hero · Why This Property · Opening stats

Dual Ambient

Tension · Energy. Warm left + cool purple right

Used in: Events · Entertainment · Attractions

CSS Implementation

All backgrounds are CSS radial-gradient compositions. No images for atmosphere. Radial halos are positioned with % coordinates. Grain texture overlay at 2–3% opacity adds material depth without visible texture.

Photography Direction

When using real Dubai Mall imagery: favor dusk/evening shots. Warm interior light against dark exterior. Long exposure for Dubai Fountain water. Architecture shots with dramatic upward perspective. Avoid overexposed daytime retail photography.
